Vision

The 412 Initiative exists to equip, train and educate as well as provide vital social, economic, and physical aid to underdeveloped global communities with the ultimate goal of providing Hope to a hurting world.

Our Mission!

Our Mission

The 412 Initiative is passionate in its mission to provide sustained Hope by facilitating positive social, economic, and physical advancement in underprivileged global communities. Through this organization, foreign communities will be better equipped to perpetuate a positive impact and transfer Hope for generations to come.
